Welcome to Book Chelve!

A virtual library consisting solely of stories that are collaborated on by the community.
There are two quirks to the site:
You will only be able to read a story after you contribute to it.
Unfortunately, you can only contribute to a story once.
Otherwise, you have a ton of freedom!
You may start a story about anything you want!
Contributions have no word limit.
You can view user profiles to see the stories that they have contributed to.
