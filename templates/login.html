Welcome to Book Chelve!

A virtual library consisting solely of stories that are collaborated on by the community.
There are two quirks to the site:

  1. Once you contribute to a story, you will be able to read the whole story.
  2. Unfortunately, you can only contribute to a story once.
Otherwise, all users have a ton of freedom.
  1. You may start a story about anything you want!
  2. Contributions have no word limit.
  3. Users can view user profiles to see the stories that they have contributed to.
